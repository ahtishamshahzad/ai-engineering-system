## What belongs here

- Reusable CI/CD, build, deploy, monitoring, and rollback patterns with trade-offs.
- Platform/tool behaviors (GitHub Actions, containers, hosting) worth remembering, with versions.
- Incident/rollback lessons with future value.

## What does NOT belong here

- Generic CI/CD tutorials — link the docs.
- Project-specific pipeline/runbook notes → `../../references/deployment/`.
- Canonical rules → `../../system/`. **No secret values — ever; templates only.**

## Quality & source requirements

- Reusable, non-obvious; cite the source (platform docs, incident postmortem).
- Record `affected_versions` for platform/tool notes; undated claims are **unverified**.
- Label trust: verified-current / project-convention / opinion / deprecated / unverified.

## Update & deprecation

- Re-verify against current platform versions; mark superseded pipeline advice **deprecated** with a successor.
